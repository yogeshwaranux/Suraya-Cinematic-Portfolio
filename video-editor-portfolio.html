FAQ

Common Questions.

What kind of work do you take on? +
I work on video editing (Reels, Shorts, YouTube, short films, commercial), photography (weddings, portraits, events), voice-over production, content strategy, and social media growth. I'm comfortable going end-to-end from brief to delivery.
What's your turnaround time for Reels? +
Standard reels are delivered within 24–48 hours. For larger batches or branded campaigns, I'll scope a weekly delivery schedule that keeps your content calendar full without compromising quality.
Do you do voice-over work? +
Yes — Tamil is my native language and I work in English conversationally. I've produced voice-overs for YouTube content, ads, and promotional videos. Studio-quality recording available on request.
Are you open to full-time roles? +
Yes, actively. I'm looking for content creator, video editor, or social media production roles — freelance or full-time. Based in Theni, Tamil Nadu, with flexibility for remote or hybrid work.
What tools do you use? +
Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Lightroom, CapCut, and Canva are my daily drivers. I also use AI tools including ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude for scripting, content planning, and research workflows.
How do I get started? +
Just reach out via email or Instagram. Tell me what you need — I'll reply within a few hours with a clear plan, timeline, and pricing. No lengthy onboarding forms, just a direct conversation.
